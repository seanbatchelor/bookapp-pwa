# Book App

A simple app to track books you want to read.

## Install on your phone

Open the link in **Safari on iPhone** (Chrome won't work for installation):

1. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of the screen
2. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
3. Tap **Add** — the app will appear on your home screen like a normal app

> Android users: open in Chrome, tap the three-dot menu, then **Add to Home Screen**

## How it works

- Tap **+** to add a book — just type the title (or title and author)
- The app will look it up automatically and fill in the details
- Tap the **circle** on the left of any book to mark it as read
- Tap a book title to see more options (mark as read/unread, delete)
- Switch to **Library** at the bottom to browse your books A–Z
